FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the bus tracking really in real-time?

Yes, our system uses GPS technology installed on each bus to provide you with up-to-the-minute location data.

Can I use the app without an account?

You can view general routes and schedules without an account. However, to save favorites and receive notifications, you will need to create a free passenger account.

What should I do if I see incorrect information?

Please use the feedback option in the passenger dashboard or our contact page to report any discrepancies. We appreciate your help in keeping our data accurate.
